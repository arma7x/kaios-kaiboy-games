Controls:
- Start button only works on main screen or to restart the game after game-over
Introduction:
Player must smash a wall of bricks by deflecting a bouncing ball with a paddle. The paddle may move horizontally and is controlled with ArrowLeft or ArrowRight. The player gets 5 lives to start with; a life is lost if the ball hits the bottom of the screen.

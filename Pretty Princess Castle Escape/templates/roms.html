Controls:
- A to Jump
- B to Attack
Introduction
The Princess has to escape from the Royal Castle killing Aznar clones, Captain Zurrapa and other deadly enemies avoiding spikes everywhere! Help her get her crown back!
The game can be beaten! This is the most important one :)
The game includes two complete levels with enemies that can kill you. Beware!
The princess can slaughter her enemies with a giant axe.
Levels have checkpoints
The game is a really hardcore classic platformer, with some difficult jumps. It can be quite enjoyable for speedrun fans and for nostalgic game boy aficionados.
